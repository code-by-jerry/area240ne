## Publishing Rules

- Publish one SEO blog every week at minimum.
- Each blog should target one primary long-tail keyword and 2-4 secondary phrases.
- Every blog should link to at least 2 relevant service pages and one conversion page like `/chat` or `/cost-estimator`.
- Prioritize Bangalore first, then Mysore, Ballari, and broader Karnataka topics.

## Editorial Checklist

- Keep SEO title under 60 characters.
- Keep SEO description between 140 and 160 characters.
- Use one clear H1 and H2-led structure in the body.
- Include at least one location modifier where relevant.
- End with a conversion CTA tied to a service page or consultation flow.
